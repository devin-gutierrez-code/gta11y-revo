# GTA11Y Revival - January 2026 Update

## What's New

GTA11Y, the accessibility mod for GTA V, has received a major update. The mod now includes comprehensive features to help blind and visually impaired players navigate Los Santos.

## Key Additions

**Navigation Assist** - A 360-degree obstacle detection system with stereo audio. Hear obstacles on your left through your left speaker, obstacles on your right through your right speaker. Different tones for pedestrians, vehicles, and walls. Works on foot and in vehicles with speed-adaptive detection range.

**Waypoint Tracking** - Set a waypoint on your map, press NumPad Decimal, and follow directional beeps to your destination. Beeps get faster as you get closer. Audio pans left/right to guide you.

**Turn-by-Turn Navigation** - While driving toward a waypoint, hear spoken directions: "Turn left in 50 meters", "Bear right", "Turn around".

**Enemy Detection** - Automatically detects hostile NPCs and plays directional audio cues toward their positions. Know where threats are coming from.

**Combat Feedback** - Audio confirmation for hits, headshots, and kills. Automatic ammo announcements when switching weapons. Low ammo warnings.

**Environmental Awareness** - Water detection with bass rumble warnings. Dropoff alerts. Door and ladder detection. Indoor/outdoor announcements. Swimming depth. Slope feedback for mountain terrain.

**Vehicle Features** - Announces vehicle name when entering. Passenger count for nearby vehicles. Health warnings for body, engine, and fuel tank damage.

**Service Proximity** - Alerts for nearby Ammu-Nations, hospitals, clothing stores, mod shops, and more.

**Customizable** - Detection radius can be set to 10m, 25m, 50m, or 100m. All new features can be toggled on/off in the settings menu.

## Quick Start

- NumPad Decimal (double-tap) - Toggle waypoint/mission tracking
- NumPad 0 - Location and cash
- Ctrl+NumPad 0 - Date and time
- NumPad Enter - Open settings menu
- NumPad 1/3 - Navigate settings
- NumPad 2 - Toggle setting

## Requirements
